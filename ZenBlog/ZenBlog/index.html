Do you take June to be your Growth partner?

Do you have 15 minutes to spare everyday for your mental Growth? I have a 30-day Mindfulness and introspection drive for the month of June. The duo is crucial practices for personal growth and well-being. Mindfulness involves bringing non-judgmental awareness to the present moment, allowing us to fully experience and appreciate our lives. It helps us cultivate self-awareness, manage stress, and enhance our ability to respond skillfully to challenging situations. Introspection, on the other hand, involves self-reflection and deep exploration of our thoughts, emotions, and experiences. It allows us to gain insights into our values, beliefs, and patterns, leading to a deeper understanding of ourselves. By engaging in mindfulness and introspection, we can develop greater clarity, resilience, and compassion towards ourselves and others, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and purposeful life.

The fate of Freelancing with the novelty of AI

I have already noticed a declining trend in the number of jobs on common freelance platforms like Upwork and Fiverr, this can only get worse as these software are continously optimized, updated and and get better every day. This is not a call for panic but to just better illustrate to which extent this could affect freelancing. Jobs like drafting YouTube scripts, Virtual assistance, Data Entry (Just to Mention a few) are slowly losing their relevance. Why would I need a Youtube script writer if I can Generate an idea in less that one minute with a ChatGPT prompt, and getting a Logo idea for my startup, I will just pop into MidJourney AI and for a few cents, I will have my brand idea for my startup ready. You can write a whole article, or a school assignment using AI, ooh and if the tone of the assignment feels machine-generated, there is another AI that can convert that text into a human-like context, this is crazy. I mean in the long run, the only thing it won't be able to replace may be Kazi ya Mjengo.
